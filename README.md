# Programming Project – Stage Monitoring Tool (Group 2)

## Introduction
In our study program, students must complete an internship (stage) as part of their education.

The internship process has several steps. These steps include submitting an internship proposal, getting approval from the internship committee, registering the internship agreement, weekly follow-up during the internship, and a final evaluation.

At the moment, this process is partly done manually and partly through different systems. Because of this, it can sometimes be confusing and it is not always clear for everyone what the current status of an internship is.

## Project Goal
To solve this problem, we want to develop a central digital application called a **Stage Monitoring Tool**.

This tool will support the full internship process and make it easier for all participants to manage and follow up information.

## Main Users
The application will be used by different people involved in the internship process:

- **Students** – submit internship proposals and weekly reports  
- **Lecturers** – follow the progress of students  
- **Company Mentors** – supervise students during the internship  
- **Internship Committee** – review and approve internship proposals  
- **Administration** – manage the system and data
